Research Interests

I study model theory, an area of mathematical logic, and its interface with topology and combinatorics.
In particular I am interested in the following topics:

  • Definable topology and functional analysis in o-minimal, dp-minimal, and other classes of tame theories.
  • Types and combinatorics in NIP theories.
